## Global Rules
- Never read, print, summarize, modify, or rely on `.env` files, `.env.*`, `secrets/`, or credential files unless I explicitly ask.
- Do not run commands that dump environment variables, such as `env`, `printenv`, or `set`.
- Do not access home-directory credential stores such as `~/.ssh`, `~/.aws`, `~/.kube`, or `~/.config/gh` unless explicitly requested.
- Prefer read-first investigation before edits.
- Prefer minimal, targeted changes over broad refactors.
